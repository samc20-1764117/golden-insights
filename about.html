About Golden Insights

Hi, I'm Sam 👋

I make Tableau tutorials that focus on the part most courses skip: making dashboards people actually enjoy using. Rounded corners, gradients, custom shapes, thoughtful interactivity — the design details that turn a wall of charts into something that feels like a modern app.

Everything here is free: nearly 200 step-by-step video tutorials, the interactive dashboards behind them on Tableau Public, and the design assets I use to build them.

The channel is co-hosted (emotionally) by Sunny, my golden retriever — the "golden" in Golden Insights. ☀️🐕
